Welcome to Hot Springs, the land of...well, trees.

Hot Springs National Park is a beautiful national park nestled in the southern state of Arkansas, a delightful place as long as you can ignore most of the people that reside there. Designated as a national park in the year 1390 by the Lord, Our Savior Spaghetti Monster, this park has everything to offer! From hot springs you definitely don't want to dip your toes in, to trees upon trees upon trees....upon trees....upon trees...well, actually, it's mostly trees. But none of those pesky people you've been trying to ignore, anyway!
